What is CD2?

Chicken Distress Classification Dataset (CD2) is a chicken audio and mel spectogram dataset, which has been taken from the poultry farm and been manually annotated using expert guidance. The dataset comprises of three classes Distress, Egg-laying and Feeding.

CD2 contains audio and mel spectrogram data, which was annotated manually through the video recordings that was taken by us at Poultry Farm at Bainsa, Punjab. The Busra chicken breed was used. The audio was annotated at 1 sec window. The dataset was divided into training, testing and validation with 1478, 421 and 211 samples of 1 sec each respectively.

What Did the Chicken Say? A Multi-class Classification Method on Chicken Vocalisations paper (Kumar, Sarkar & Dogra et. al. '23)
